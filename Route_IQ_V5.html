About RouteIQ Fleet

Multi-vehicle route optimization platform with intelligent fuel management

Fleet Management

RouteIQ supports multiple vehicles with intelligent cluster-based assignment. Each vehicle is automatically routed to its nearest delivery cluster, with cross-vehicle compensation when fuel reserves run low.

Fuel Optimization

Each vehicle operates within its designated cluster. When fuel drops below 30% with more than 10 stops remaining, nearby vehicles automatically take over deliveries.

Interactive Map

Click anywhere on the map to instantly set vehicle depot locations. No manual coordinate entry required. Select a vehicle and click to position it anywhere in the coverage zone.

Pareto Optimal

The optimization engine finds the ideal balance between delivery time and fuel consumption, ensuring neither metric can be improved without sacrificing the other.
